Tourist Spots in Rizal

Explore nature, art, and history just outside Metro Manila

Masungi Georeserve

A conservation area with rope bridges, limestone formations, and forest trails.

Antipolo Cathedral

A pilgrimage site known for the image of Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage.

Pinto Art Museum

A contemporary art space with Mediterranean-style architecture and gardens.

Daranak Falls

A popular nature getaway with waterfalls, picnic areas, and fresh mountain air.

Hinulugang Taktak

A historic waterfall and national park perfect for family day trips and nature lovers.
